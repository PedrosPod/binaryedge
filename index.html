About us

We created BinaryEdge with the vision that we could establish an ecosystem of security services and products that would allow our clients to be agile, rapid and yet secure.

Our team, having previously experienced how security can be a hurdle on both software development or infrastructure deployment, decided that it was time to bring security standards up-to-date with the agile software development methodologies.

Using our ecosystem of tools and services, our clients will be able to secure their customers and their data without having to worry about security being a blocker on their development.

Red Team Engagement

A red team is a type of engagement that should be attempted by organizations which either have highly classified information on their ecosystem or that are highly confident in the efficiency of their security levels and wish to put them to a test. This type of engagement will provide the client with a large scale test that will attempt to break and penetrate all defenses of your ecosystem.

In this type of engagement, the Red Team from BinaryEdge will work as actual attackers and try to obtain control and access all information that your security is trying to defend. In the typical types of engagements, only digital systems are tested; in red teaming social, engineering and physical defenses are also put to a test.

This type of engagement is perfect for you, as a client, to gain knowledge, such as:

  • How are your incident response systems and processes working against real-world threats? How can you improve them?
  • Gain deeper understanding of the current flaws that exist in your ecosystem.
  • Fill voids in your security training and awareness programs for both technical and non-technical people.
  • Identify new attack vectors and threats that may affect you.
